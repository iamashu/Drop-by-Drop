          Madam Grace Pinto (Managing Director, Ryan Group of Institutions)

          Madam Grace Pinto
          Madam Grace Pinto, the Managing Director of St. Xavier's and Ryan International Schools is synonymous with excellence, expertise, experience and all that goes into the moulding of young minds in a systematic way to enable them to play their role effectively. She believes that grooming every child entrusted to our care, chiseling his talents and empowering him academically is very important. Preparing him socially with the help of the parents and teachers to give him emotional security and strength will help him to fly out into the broad expanse of this Universe to lead a life of dignity and success with God's blessings. Madam Pinto is a constant star of support and guidance for the Principals and teachers. She is the one who motivates them towards pinnacles of glory. The warmth in her embraces makes their day memorable and inspires them to attain the yet unattained and leave their footprints in the sands of time as true Ryanites. For her invaluable contribution to the field of education and to the society, she is honored  with countless prestigious awards.
