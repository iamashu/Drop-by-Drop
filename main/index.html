          No water ! No Life ! 
          The next World War will be for water !

          India is one of the wettest countries in the world and yet facing the water problem. It receives 400 million hectares precipitation, primarily as rain which alone is sufficient to meet most of the demands. Still many parts of rural and urban India face drought like situation. 

          Water, one of our most valuable resource must be saved by all from it’s
          • Overuse
          • And Lack of management

          The site highlights the reasons of water scarcity in tropical and sub-tropical regions and suggests easy to implement solutions for it.

          Our Research Program works at 4 stages:
          • Collection Of data
          • Identification Of the Problems
          • Finding out easy to implement solutions for the problem
          • Spreading awareness about the problems and solutions worldwide
